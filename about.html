About Us

The Lilongwe Academy International School

Welcome to The Lilongwe Academy International School - TLAIS, a leading institution offering the globally recognized Cambridge curriculum across all educational stages, from Early Childhood to Upper Secondary. At TLAIS, we are dedicated to nurturing the academic, personal, and social development of every student, preparing them for success in a rapidly changing world.

Our school provides a seamless educational journey, incorporating the following key stages:

  • Cambridge Early Years: Our Early Years program offers a play-based, child-centered approach to learning, focusing on the holistic development of young learners. We create a nurturing and stimulating environment where children can explore their interests, build social skills, and develop a strong foundation for future learning.
  • Cambridge Primary: In the Primary section, we foster a love for learning by providing a solid grounding in core subjects such as English, Mathematics, and Science, while also encouraging creative expression and personal growth. Our learners are guided to think critically, collaborate, and gain confidence as they progress through the key stages.
  • Cambridge Lower Secondary: The Lower Secondary curriculum continues to build on the knowledge and skills developed in Primary, preparing students for the challenges ahead. We emphasize independent thinking, analytical reasoning, and global awareness, laying the groundwork for success in more advanced academic pursuits.
  • Cambridge Upper Secondary: As students move into Upper Secondary, they embark on a more specialized academic path with the Cambridge IGCSE curriculum. This internationally recognized program enables them to study a broad range of subjects, preparing them for higher education and future careers. Our focus on academic excellence, leadership, and personal development equips students with the skills to thrive in the modern world.
  • Cambridge GCE (AS/A Levels): Our post-16 education offers the prestigious Cambridge International AS and A Levels, providing students with the in-depth knowledge and skills required for university and beyond. These qualifications are highly respected by universities worldwide and offer students a strong foundation for further academic and professional success.

At TLAIS, we pride ourselves on fostering a culture of respect, integrity, and lifelong learning. Our experienced and passionate educators are dedicated to helping students reach their full potential, while our supportive community ensures that every child feels valued and encouraged.

We invite you to join us in making the 2024-2025 academic year one of discovery, growth, and achievement for all our learners.
