Welcome

The Lilongwe Academy International School

Dear Parents, Guardians, Students, and Staff, It is with great excitement and anticipation that I welcome you all to the start of the 2024-2025 academic year at The Lilongwe International School. Whether you are part of our Cambridge Early Years center, Cambridge Primary School, or High School Section, we are thrilled to embark on this journey of learning, growth, and achievement together.

For our youngest learners in Cambridge Early Years, this year marks the beginning of an exciting adventure where curiosity, creativity, and exploration are at the heart of every day. We are committed to nurturing their natural love of learning and fostering an environment that is warm, supportive, and filled with joy.

In Primary School, students will continue to build strong foundations across academic subjects, while developing critical thinking, collaboration, and leadership skills. Our dedicated team is here to guide and support each child, encouraging them to explore their unique talents and potential.

As our High School students progress toward the future, they will be encouraged to take on new challenges, deepen their knowledge, and prepare for success in higher education and beyond. With a wide range of academic and extracurricular opportunities, we aim to foster not only academic excellence but also a sense of responsibility, independence, and community engagement.

At every level of our school, we are dedicated to creating an environment that promotes respect, inclusion, and a love for lifelong learning. We encourage parents and guardians to stay actively involved in their childrenâ€™s education, as your support is vital to their success.

Let us work together to make this academic year one of inspiration, growth, and achievement for all. I look forward to seeing each of you thrive in the months ahead.

Warm regards,

Yohani Kasambara

Principal
